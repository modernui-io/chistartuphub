How to Use This Guide

Think of your startup like a house. You wouldn't install the roof before pouring the foundation. This atlas helps you identify which "floor" you're building and what support beams need strengthening.

Diagnostic Mode

Find where you are today. Identify gaps in your foundation. See what's holding you back.

Roadmap Mode

See what comes next. Prepare for future challenges. Build ahead of your needs.

Phase 1: Foundation Testing if the problem is real
Product — Problem-Fit
The Question: Do people actually have this problem?

You're Here If:

  • You're talking to potential customers weekly
  • You have a hypothesis but no paying customers yet
  • Your "product" might just be a landing page or prototype

Warning Signs

Building features no one asked for. Avoiding customer conversations. Assuming you know what people want.

Team — Solo/Co-founders
The Question: Do you have the right people to start this journey?
  • 1-2 founders doing everything
  • Roles are fluid, everyone does everything
  • No formal structure yet

Action Items

  • Draft co-founder agreement (equity, roles, vesting)
  • Identify your #1 skill gap
  • Define who makes final decisions in each area
Funding

Self-funded or friends & family. <$100K raised. 6-12 months runway.

Phase 2: Traction Making it repeatable
Product — Product-Market Fit
The Question: Do people love this enough to pay and tell others?
  • Revenue is growing (even if small)
  • Users are sticking around (retention > 40%)
  • Word-of-mouth is happening

Warning Signs

Users sign up but don't come back. Sales require heavy discounting. Every customer wants something different.

Team — Core Team (3-10)
The Question: Can you build a team that executes without you in every meeting?
  • First 3-5 employees hired
  • Some specialization emerging
  • Starting to think about culture

Action Items

  • Document your values (actually live them)
  • Create onboarding process
  • Delegate one major area completely

Phase 3: Scale Doing it 10x bigger
Product — Expansion

Core product stable. Exploring adjacent markets. Building platform plays.

Warning

Don't expand before core is solid.

Team — Organization

20+ employees. Multiple management layers. Formal HR and operations.

Warning

Systems scale without linear headcount growth.

The Diagnostic Tool

Answer honestly for each dimension:

Product

  • Can you describe your ICP in one sentence?
  • What's your activation & retention rate?
  • Where does growth come from?

Team

  • Who makes final decisions in each area?
  • How long until new hire is productive?
  • What if a key person leaves?

Funding

  • How many months of runway?
  • What milestones unlock next funding?
  • What's your CAC and LTV?

Operations

  • Can someone else do your job for a week?
  • How long to onboard new employee?
  • Where do you waste the most time?

Founder Focus Checklist

Weekly

  • Talk to at least 3 customers
  • Review key metrics dashboard
  • One-on-ones with direct reports
  • Update investors (if applicable)

Monthly

  • Full team all-hands
  • Financial review (cash position)
  • Competitive landscape check
  • Personal energy audit

Quarterly

  • OKR/goal setting
  • Board meeting (if applicable)
  • Team retrospective
  • Strategic planning session
