## THE CLOSURE

### Aqua Cultured Foods Shuts Down

**Chicago's alt-seafood startup Aqua Cultured Foods closed after 5 years**, despite:
- Raising $10M+ in funding
- Filing 19 patents for fish-free seafood technology
- Developing fermentation-based seafood alternatives

**Why it matters:** A reminder that even well-funded companies with strong IP can struggle with market timing and capital efficiency in deeptech/foodtech. The founders contributed significant innovation to the space.
