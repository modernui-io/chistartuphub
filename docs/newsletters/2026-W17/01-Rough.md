### Madison Air IPO | $2.23B

**Sector:** Public offering — industrial sector

Madison Air, a spinout of Larry Gies' Chicago-based buyout firm Madison Industries, has successfully priced its IPO, marking the largest industrial IPO in nearly 27 years.

**Close Date:** 2026-04-15

### Achieve Partners Fund | $450M

**Sector:** Education investment focused on apprenticeships

Achieve Partners has raised a $450 million fund aimed at addressing labor displacement due to artificial intelligence in technology and healthcare sectors, emphasizing on-the-job training.

**Close Date:** 2026-04-20
